WHO WE ARE
Lamar Lighting is a company specialized in the field of lighting founded in Amman – Jordan 2013, the company provides wide range of luminaires units that are selected carefully according to international standards and specifications.

Our products include indoor and outdoor luminaires with different design patterns to ensure the most advanced and energy-efficient lighting solution on a continuous basis To meet the needs of factories, offices, hospitals and garden lighting And houses, streets, etc.

We dedicate ourselves to develop and improve the quality of products to satisfy our valued customers as we are committed to bringing the best products to the market we are proud to be known as a trusted partner providing customers with insight and invaluable support.
