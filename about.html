WHO WE ARE
Lemar Lighting is a company specialized in the lighting field, founded in Amman – Jordan 2013, providing a wide range of luminaires carefully selected according to international standards and specifications.

Our products include indoor and outdoor luminaires with different design patterns, offering the most advanced and energy-efficient lighting solutions.

We dedicate ourselves to continually develop and improve product quality to satisfy our valued customers. We are committed to bringing the best products to market and proud to be a trusted partner providing insight and invaluable support.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, sustainable, and tailored lighting solutions that enhance spaces and reduce energy costs for our partners and customers, adhering to the highest global standards.

Our Vision

To be the trusted partner leading the lighting market in Jordan and the region by innovation, quality, and genuine customer care.
At Lemar Lighting, we provide efficient, sustainable, and tailored lighting solutions that enhance spaces and reduce energy costs. Since 2013, we’ve been helping businesses, architects, and designers worldwide create smarter, more impactful environments with lighting that meets the highest global standards.
